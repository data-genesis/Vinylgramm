Grand Ole Opry members, Grammy nominees, and multi-award awards, Dailey & Vincent will be releasing their brand-new album- A Beautiful Life on Pillar Stone Records. This new album will feature twelve new tracks including the #1 Bluegrass Radio song- "Ruby" as well as "Longer Than That" which was written by Tenille Townes & Park Chisolm. Dailey & Vincent are among the most acclaimed duos in modern American roots music. With unmatched harmonies and musicianship, Jamie Dailey and Darrin Vincent have built a career that bridges bluegrass, country, and gospel with heart, humor, and faith. The album will be available on CD and available on limited edition vinyl.
