Originally released in 2021 and unavailable on Vinyl for years, “Altars” is a remix album by the Texas-based darkwave duo Twin Tribes. It features reimagined tracks from their first two studio albums, Shadows (2018) and Ceremony (2019). The collection showcases diverse interpretations from prominent artists in the modern post-punk and darkwave scene, including She Past Away, Ash Code, Bootblacks, Wingtips, and Creux Lies, highlighting the band's influence and versatility within the underground music community.
