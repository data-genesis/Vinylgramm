description:
JuJu Rogers’ forthcoming project marks a bold new chapter in his artistic evolution, situated at the intersection of music, culture, and social commentary. Rooted in Afrop(h)unk and inspired by the psychedelic funk of Parliament-Funkadelic, the project seeks to sonically reconnect the Black Diaspora with its African origins. It reflects on the history of the Buffalo Soldiers and draws parallels between their experiences, JuJu’s own journey, and the broader realities of Black life. Influenced by the spirit of resistance embodied by the Maroons, as well as radical left skinhead and rudeboy culture, the project challenges racial stereotypes and promotes a decolonized self-perception through a contemporary Afrofuturist framework.Musically, the album is expansive and experimental, featuring collaborators such as Pink Siifu, MONEYNICCA of Soul Glo, and Jamila Woods. It blends punk, hardcore, hip-hop, soul, rock, and funk into a genre-defying soundscape. Alongside its political and cultural themes, the project is deeply personal, addressing self-love, vulnerability, the tension between outward joy and inner struggle and represents a sonic, spiritual, and physical form of liberation.
