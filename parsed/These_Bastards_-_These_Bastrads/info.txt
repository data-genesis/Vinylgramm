description:
A crazy tripped-out-thrash hymn worshipping at the altar of the West Bay Koalition. Imagine a mix between Zappa, Kalmaex, And The Riff Merchants, Spazz, The Feederz, with a touch of Captain Beefheart, executed across 16 tracks of high-speed punk thrash. One of the craziest concept HC punk LPs to come out of the West Bay, listening to this will make you want to fight in your local Jack-in-the-Box parking lot. Features ex-members of Three Wheel Motion, Venkman, Agents OF Satan, Slobber, Morbid Life Society, VVM, Conquest FOR Death and gazillion other West Bay / Bay Area projects, fronted by the legendary Leech of Slobber. Recorded by the high priest of all things West Bay, Bart Thurber at House of Faith Studios. Drink the fucking Kool-Aid! 625330. Limited to 250, all on green vinyl. Co-release with Alimentary Music, Coxinha and Mighty Morphine Power Violence.
