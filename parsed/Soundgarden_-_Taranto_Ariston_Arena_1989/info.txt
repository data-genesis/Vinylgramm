description:
This concert recorded in Taranto (Italy) the 7th May 1989 was one of the last dates of their first european tour promoted by legendary label SST Records. Altough Soundgarden was few known, especially in south Italy, this event will be talked about a lot when with “Superunknown” they will be international stars. The audio was taken directly by the soundboard and remastered for the vinyl pressing in an extra limited edition of 300 copie on black vinyl. The cove was made using a promotional photo by SST (the same used for single “Flower” ) autographed by all the band members on the day of the Taranto concert. The photos on the back cover was also taken from the same concert
