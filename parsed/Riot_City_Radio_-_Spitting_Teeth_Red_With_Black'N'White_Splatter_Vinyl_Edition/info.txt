description:
Shout it out loud: We are the underground! The working class is fed up, they go out, start the revolution, dance and sometimes fight to the beat of RIOT CITY RADIO's songs. Are these clichés? Of course. But every punk rock & Oi! has always been based on clichés. Just like any other subculture. So it's not a bad thing. On the contrary, it's fun. Especially with RIOT CITY RADIO, because their songs are instantly catchy anthems with perfectly balanced songwriting between powerful chorals, awesome singalongs and with raw, violent edge, and an addictive factor! An ode to subculture, underdogs and solidarity, distancing oneself from this sick society and all its unnecessary excesses. And RCR mean every single word of their songs and have a lot to say! With their second album 'Spitting Teeth,' the band from Plymouth,UK blasts off with a charismatic street punk and Oi! explosion! Music with skill, hard punch, and a kick-ass street attitude. 11 new songs incl. the singles "We re still singing" and "Underground" On ltd black and ltd multicolored vinyl!
