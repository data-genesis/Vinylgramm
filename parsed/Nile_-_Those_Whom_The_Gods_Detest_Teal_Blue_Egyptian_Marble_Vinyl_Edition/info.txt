description:
Nile's technical death metal monument returns: Those Whom The Gods Detest receives the ultimate reissue treatment!Following 2007's Ithyphallic, Nile's 2009 masterpiece Those Whom The Gods Detest is widely regarded as a pinnacle of technical death metal. This re-issue captures the relentless, high-quality sonic assault that solidified Nile as titans of the genre. Produced by Neil Kernon and featuring stellar drumming from George Kollias, the album is a perfectly balanced attack of brutal, intellectual death metal.Pressed on stunning "Teal Blue Egyptian Marble" effect vinyl, echoing the deep, ominous colors of the Nile river at twilight and opulent Egyptian stone.Features custom artwork etching on the final side and presented in a high-quality gatefold sleeve w/obi strip.
