“Woradj Alle-Impressions of Ethiopia for solo violin and electronics”, is the first solo album by New York based Kaethe Hostetter. After calling Ethiopia home for eleven years, K8A has put together a beautiful seven song album. No words, no drums, no back up musicians, nothing but K8A with violin and pedals creating a calming and warm audio experience to be added to the Ethiopian canon of music. Comes in a tip-on Stoughton sleeve with insert.
