description:
Sound of Sexy Soul is the second studio album by the American Philadelphia soul group The Delfonics. Released in 1969, the album blends lush string arrangements, gentle horn sections, and the group’s signature falsetto harmonies to create a romantic, intimate atmosphere that became their hallmark. Songs on the record move seamlessly between tender ballads and mid-tempo grooves, highlighting both emotive vocals and polished, melodic production. Sound of Sexy Soul exemplifies The Delfonics’ ability to fuse sensuality, elegance, and musical craftsmanship, solidifying their influence on the development of 1970s soul and later R&B. The album opens with the single "Ready or Not Here I Come (Can't Hide from Love)", later covered, sampled and interpolated by many famous artists in numerous songs. This vinyl reissue is the first since 1969.
