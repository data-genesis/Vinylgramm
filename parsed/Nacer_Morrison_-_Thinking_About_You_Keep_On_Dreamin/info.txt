PPU's first pseudonymic release. Recognition aside, we first stumbled upon Nacer's tracks on Diva Radio France two decades ago. Over the years, the songs have been ripped, uploaded, wantlisted, and widely shared. But wait—was there even a record (no), who broke the vocoder? Turns out the artist was someone we knew all along, had even bought records off PPU since the very beginning. Nacer wishes to maintain that air of mystery, allowing this music to forever thrive just as it has for so many years and will maintain... dedicated to the forever search for obscure funk music and their creators!
