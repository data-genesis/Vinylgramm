description:
Souxsoul, the production project of Swiss DJ and producer Mario Held, has been active in the international house scene since the late 1990s. His work is strongly influenced by the golden era of disco and funk from the late seventies and early eighties, which gives his productions a warm, soulful character with grooves that translate effortlessly to the dancefloor. Over the years, his music has appeared on several respected international labels and continues to find its way into DJ sets and radio shows around the world. Groovefonic complements this musical language with a slightly more contemporary club-oriented perspective. His productions combine deep house structures, disco-inspired grooves and elegant basslines, creating tracks that work equally well in refined DJ sets and on energetic dancefloors. Together they have recently released a limited vinyl pressing where each side presents a brand-new production by one of the two artists. The idea behind this release was to unite two distinct yet closely related sonic identities on a single record while celebrating the enduring culture of vinyl, something that remains particularly meaningful for DJs and collectors alike.
