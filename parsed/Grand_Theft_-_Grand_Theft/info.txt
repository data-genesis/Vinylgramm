Grand Theft was a one-shot explosion of heavy rock madness out of Seattle in 1972. What began as a tongue-in-cheek jab at the bombast of bands like Grand Funk Railroad quickly spiraled into something raw, real, and entirely their own. Dave Baroh's snarling guitar riffs, Kevin Marin's thunderous bass, and Phil klitgaard's primal yet sharp drumming cut through like a cracking whip on a stormy night.Their sole LP, recorded in a single chaotic session, captures a moment of unfiltered energy.
