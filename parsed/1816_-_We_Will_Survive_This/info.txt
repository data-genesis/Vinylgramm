With "We Will Survive This," the acclaimed ensemble 1816 continues its exploration of vulnerability, resilience and quiet hope. Vocalist Wenche Losnegard, bassist and principal composer Audun Ellingsen, cellist Gregor Riddell and drummer Erik Nylander expand the sonic and emotional landscape introduced on their celebrated debut, blending elements of chamber music, jazz and alternative pop into a cohesive, deeply expressive whole.The band"s name, referencing "the year without summer" (1816), serves as a thematic backdrop to the album"s reflections on instability, deprivation and survival in times when familiar structures seem to dissolve.
