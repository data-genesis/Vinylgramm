UK Beatdown royalty returns after 9 years, featuring new vocalist Tony Evans (Idle Hands) and mixed by Will Putney (Knocked Loose, Counterparts). Finding Peace is a 23-minute, no-nonsense sledgehammer of raw aggression, delivering the band's heaviest, most refined sound to date.After over 5 years of long-distance writing, squeezed in between demanding full time work commitments, a billion breakdowns in the form of the Whatsapp-hummed voice note, 30 plus Toby carveries and countless hurdles; including the monumental task of switching lead vocalist, we're mega proud to announce the completion of Desolated's long awaited new full length album.Featuring guest spots from Ninebar (London, UK) Whispers (Bangkok, Thailand), and D bloc (Detroit, MI). We are proud to say this album has more of a heavy hardcore twist than previously and is our best work to date. With the honour of legend Will Putney handling the mix and master - we are excited to show what gentrified Desolated sounds like.
